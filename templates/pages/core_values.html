IPEKA Core Values

I. Mission

To educate students holistically based on Christian values.

II. Vision

To become a leading Christian educational institution that proclaims Christ as the source of wisdom and knowledge.

III. Culture and Organisational Values

  1. Service
  2. Discipline
  3. Integritas
  4. Lifelong learner
  5. Respect for all individuals

IV. Motto

Better Education in Science and Truth (BEST)

V. Expected Schoolwide Learning Results (ESLRs)

  1. Know God as the creator, owner, redeemer, and sustainer.
  2. Know God as the source of wisdom and knowledge and the highest moral standard.
  3. Know oneself correctly and cultivate the knowledge and talents bestowed by God to fulfill their life purpose.
  4. Possess a Christian character and perspective in studying every aspect of knowledge and daily life.
  5. Have the ability to solve problems, think critically, creatively, innovatively, and skillfully utilise technology according to God's wisdom.
  6. Have effective communication and collaboration skills as a global community member, making a positive impact on the environment.
